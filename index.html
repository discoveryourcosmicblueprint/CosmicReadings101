About Your Reading

Understanding the ancient sciences behind your cosmic blueprint

🔢

Numerology

Numerology is the ancient study of numbers and their cosmic vibrations. Your birth date and name carry specific numerical frequencies that reveal your life purpose, personality traits, soul desires, and destiny path. We calculate five core numbers: Life Path, Destiny, Soul Urge, Personality, and Birthday numbers, each offering unique insights into different aspects of your being.

⭐

Astrology

Astrology maps the positions of celestial bodies at your exact moment of birth, creating a unique cosmic fingerprint. Beyond your Sun sign, we analyze your Moon sign (emotions), Rising sign (outer self), planetary placements, house positions, aspects between planets, and special points like the Nodes and Chiron, providing a complete picture of your cosmic influences.

🌈

Chakra System

The seven chakras are energy centers within your subtle body, each governing different aspects of physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Based on your astrological and numerological profile, we analyze the state and balance of each chakra, offering insights into your energetic health and providing guidance for alignment and healing.

🎴

Destiny Matrix

The Destiny Matrix system combines numerology with the 22 Major Arcana of the Tarot, revealing the archetypal energies guiding your life. Each position in your matrix represents different life areas—from your soul purpose to karmic lessons, talents, challenges, and spiritual evolution—creating a comprehensive map of your destiny.
